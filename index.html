About Adi

Every piece begins as a lump of earth and a quiet idea. Adi works with stoneware and porcelain in a small studio filled with natural light, shaping each vessel by hand on the wheel or through patient coil-building. There is no rush here — only the slow conversation between hands and clay, glaze and flame. The results are objects meant to be touched, held, and lived with — bowls that cradle your morning coffee, vases that lean slightly because perfection was never the point.
